Established in 2024, our NGO is dedicated to providing access to quality education for underprivileged children in Nepal. We were founded with a simple but powerful mission: to break the barriers that prevent children from accessing education and to empower them with the tools they need to build a brighter future. We believe that education is a fundamental right, not a privilege, and it is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty and transforming lives.

Our work focuses on funding school fees, providing learning materials, and offering support to ensure that children not only attend school but thrive academically. We work closely with communities to identify children in need and provide them with the necessary resources to stay in school, from early education through secondary levels. By partnering with local schools, teachers, and families, we create a strong support system that encourages students to achieve their full potential.

Since our inception, we have positively impacted the lives of many children across Nepal. Our Key Initiatives

The Raising Hands Foundation aims to break the cycle of poverty and empower children to achieve their full potential, ultimately contributing to healthier, more educated communities.

Tutoring and Mentorship

Connecting students with mentors and tutors who can provide guidance, support, and academic assistance.

After-School Programs

Creating safe and engaging environments for children to learn and grow outside of regular school hours, focusing on both academic and personal development.

Infrastructure Improvement

Working to improve school facilities and resources, ensuring children have access to a conducive learning environment.

Scholarships and Financial Aid

Offering financial assistance to help children from low-income families afford school tuition, books, and supplies.

Community Engagement

Involving families and communities in educational initiatives to foster a supportive atmosphere for children's learning.

Raising Hands Foundation is a non-profit organization established in 2024, dedicated to providing access to quality education for underprivileged children in Western Nepal.
