After-School Programs

  1. Home
  2. After-School Programs

Our After-School Program provides a safe and supportive environment for children to continue learning and growing beyond regular school hours. We offer a range of activities that blend academic enrichment with creative and recreational opportunities. From homework assistance and interactive learning sessions to arts, sports, and leadership development activities, this program is designed to help students explore their interests while reinforcing important life skills. By engaging in structured yet fun activities, children not only improve their academic performance but also develop social skills, teamwork, and self-discipline. The After-School Program at Raising Hands Foundation ensures that every child has a productive, enriching space to spend their afternoons, fostering their overall growth and well-being.

Raising Hands Foundation is a non-profit organization established in 2024, dedicated to providing access to quality education for underprivileged children in Western Nepal.
