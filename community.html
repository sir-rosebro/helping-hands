Community Engagement

  1. Home
  2. Community Engagement

We believe that lasting change is only possible when the entire community is involved. Our Community Engagement initiatives are designed to foster collaboration between local residents, parents, educators, and volunteers to create a supportive network that empowers children and promotes educational development. We actively encourage community participation in our programs, from contributing to school infrastructure improvements to volunteering in mentorship and after-school activities.

By involving the community in decision-making and implementation, we aim to build a sense of ownership and shared responsibility for the success of our initiatives. Parents, teachers, and community leaders play a crucial role in shaping the educational experience of the children, ensuring that the programs we implement are tailored to local needs and have a lasting impact. This collaborative approach also helps build trust, strengthen relationships, and create a collective commitment to the well-being and future of the children.

Our Community Engagement efforts extend beyond education. We organize workshops, awareness campaigns, and events that promote social issues such as health, hygiene, environmental sustainability, and gender equality. By addressing these broader concerns, we aim to create a holistic environment where children can thrive. Through active community involvement, we can build stronger, more resilient societies where education is valued and supported by everyone.

Raising Hands Foundation is a non-profit organization established in 2024, dedicated to providing access to quality education for underprivileged children in Western Nepal.
