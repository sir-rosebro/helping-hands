Our partners empower us to provide education to children in poverty and expand our reach.

Together we can do so much more.

Children growing up in poverty face challenges that no child should have to endure. Together, we can break the cycle and create a future where every child has the opportunity to dream, learn, and succeed. We strongly believe that partnerships have the power to amplify our impact. From the outset, we have worked alongside local partners to collaboratively support communities in West Nepal through education and empowerment. We remain dedicated to fostering these valuable partnerships, along with other strategic alliances with like-minded organizations, to maximize our efficiency and the impact of our poverty alleviation efforts in the communities we serve.

Giving back to the community is an essential aspect of responsible entrepreneurship. Business owners have a unique opportunity to impact their local areas positively. We would especially like to thank the following organizations for their partnership and support in empowering children living in poverty through education, as well as the various trusts and foundations that prefer to remain anonymous.
