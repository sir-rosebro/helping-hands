Tutoring And Mentorship

  1. Home
  2. Tutoring And Mentorship

At the Raising Hands Foundation, we understand that personalized attention and academic support can significantly impact a child's learning journey. Our tutoring programs are designed to provide one-on-one or small group instruction tailored to the specific needs of each student. By offering guidance in subjects where students may struggle, we help them build a strong academic foundation, boost their confidence, and improve their overall performance in school. Our dedicated tutors are passionate about making learning accessible and engaging, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to succeed, regardless of their circumstances.

Our mentorship program goes beyond academics, focusing on holistic development and personal growth. We connect students with experienced mentors who provide guidance, encouragement, and advice on overcoming challenges both inside and outside the classroom. By fostering meaningful relationships, we aim to inspire children to dream big, set goals, and pursue their aspirations. Our mentors serve as role models, helping students develop essential life skills, build resilience, and cultivate a sense of self-worth. Through this program, we empower young learners to navigate their educational journey and prepare for a bright future.

Raising Hands Foundation is a non-profit organization established in 2024, dedicated to providing access to quality education for underprivileged children in Western Nepal.
